Concept of using this service is very simple!





1. First of all, you have to create telegram channel for your group and add student to it.

3. Fill the form and press a button «Generate a link». Pay attention to the last field of form Telegram channel. You have to paste a channel name in format @channel_name.



4. After the link is generated you can copy it or send it to the telegram channel.
